Common Exercises

The following are common exercises that we encourage our clients to do as part of their daily exercise routine

Burpee
Burpees are a great, full body exercise to increase your strength and endurance. Begin in a standing position, drop into a squat and extend forward again quickly, and then jump up from a squatted position.
Plank
Planks build your core strength. To preform a plank, get in a push up position and rest your forearms on the floor. Hold the position as long as you can
Mountain Climber
Mountain climbers are a good cardio exercise. Place your hands on the floor in a push up position, then bring one knee up to your chest and then swich as quickly as you can. (As if you're climbing a mountain
